Gibson Epiphone Limited Edition Les Paul Custom
AU $635.25

2008 Epiphone Limited Edition Custom Shop Les Paul Custom Electric Guitar in excellent condition. Classic Silverburst finish with white binding, chrome hardware, black pickguard and Rosewood fingerboard with mother of pearl block inlays. The guitar is set up with a nice action and the slim taper "D" profile neck has a great feel. The frets are in excellent condition. The pickups have a good tone with nice output. The back of the headstock has been stamped with a 2nd. We cannot tell what the reason for this is. The guitar has no visible defects -- Looks, plays and sounds great. Includes gig bag.

  • Fretboard: rosewood
  • Frets: 22 Medium jumbo
  • Bridge: Tune-o-matic with LockTone™ Stopbar tailpiece
  • Tuners: grover.
