Fender Squier MIJ Stratocaster
AU $789

This guitar comes from a unique and underrated part of Fender’s history where surprisingly good quality guitars were leaving the Japanese factory stamped with the Squier name. Modern players are continuing to notice the exceptional bang-for-the-buck quality of these guitars, which look, play, and sound like their Fender counterparts. This guitar has a distinct vintage vibe between the slight tint the neck has sustained from graceful aging, the light overall wear, and the classic pickups. In addition, this guitar is in fantastic shape for its age and still plays like champ.

Features

  • Body: Double cutaway contoured body in Red. 1-ply white pickgaurd. Synchronized tremolo bridge with individually adjustable saddles. White/parchment top hat knobs. White switch tip.
  • Neck: C shape maple neck and maple fingerboard with black dot inlays. 1 5/8” nut width. Chrome Fender sealed tuning machines.
  • Pickups/Electronics: • Electronics: Standard single-coil pickups. 1 x volume, 2 x tone controls. 5-way pickup selector.
  • Weight: 8 lbs 4 oz
  • Case: Includes the original hardshell case. Some small accessories are included in the case.
  • Playability/Sound The C shape maple neck on this guitar will be familiar to most and comfortable to just as many. With the setup our techs have given it, this guitar plays great. The single-coils have the classic Strat sound and still span a good range of tones.
