About us

Virgin Guitars is a musical instrument boutique located in Lismore, New South Wales. It was established in 2005 by Ma and Pa Duck who have been in the music business for the last forty years.

Specializing in new and quality used guitars, the store maintains a comprehensive stock of instruments available for immediate delivery.
