Jackson KVMG Pro Series King V
AU $1255

The regally commanding KVMG Pro Series King V features an alder body, 3-piece through-body maple neck with graphite reinforcement and hand-rubbed oil finish, compound-radius (12"-16") ebony fingerboard with 24 jumbo frets and alumiloid shark fin inlays, white neck and headstock binding, EMG 81 (bridge) and EMG 89 with push/pull volume coil tap and 3-way toggle switching, Floyd Rose bridge, black hardware and Dunlop strap locks.

Features

  • Body:
    • Body Material: Alder
    • Body Finish: Gloss
    • Body Shape: King V
    • Body Top: Maple Veneer
  • Neck:
    • Neck Material: 3-Piece Maple with Graphite Reinforcement
    • Neck Finish: Oiled
    • Neck Shape: Speed Neck
    • Neck Binding: 1-Ply White
    • Scale Length: 25.5" (648 mm)
  • Fingerboard:
    • Fingerboard Radius: 12" to 16" Compound Radius (304.8 mm to 406.4 mm)
    • Number of Frets: 24
    • Fret Size: Jumbo
    • String Nut: Floyd Rose R3 Locking
    • Nut Width: 1.6875" (42.8 mm)
  • Position inlays: Alumiloid Shark Fin
  • Truss rod: Dual action
  • Head stock: Binding: 1-Ply White
  • Headstock: Jackson Pointed 6-In-Line
  • Electronics:
    • Bridge Pickup: Direct Mount EMG 81
    • Neck Pickup: Direct Mount EMG 89
    • Controls: Master Volume, Master Tone
    • Pickup Switching: 3-Position Toggle: Up: Neck Pickup, Middle: Bridge and Neck
    • Pickups, Down: Bridge Pickup
    • Pickup Configuration: HH
  • Hardware:
    • Bridge: Floyd Rose FRT-O2000 Double-Locking Tremolo (Recessed)
    • Hardware Finish: Black
    • Tuning Machines: Jackson Sealed Die-Cast
    • Control Knobs: Knurled Dome
    • Switch Tips: Black
