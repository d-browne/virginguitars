FAQ

Here you can find some of the most frequently asked questions on Virgin Guitar!

Q: Do the guitars comes with cases?

A: Every guitar comes with its original hard shell case or a quality after market case.

Q: What is your Virgin Guitars' policy on warranty and returns?

A: As well as the one year manufacturers’ warranty for new guitars, Virgin Guitars will refund the purchase price if for any reason you are not happy with your guitar within seven days of its receipt.

Q: What forms of payment does Virgin Guitars' accept?

A: Virgin Guitars accepts payment by PayPal or Bitcoin which can be selected at Checkout.

Q: How are the prodcuts delivered and what are the shipping costs?

A: Virgin Guitars delivers anywhere in Australia by StarTrak Express for a flat fee of $50.00 which will be automatically added to the cost at Checkout.
